- **Benchmark is ChatGPT:** Immediately and repeatedly compares BellaAssist features to what she can already do with ChatGPT. The free availability of ChatGPT is her primary commercial concern for BellaAssist.
- **Table stakes vs value-add distinction:** Document intelligence (summary, quality grading) = table stakes ("I can utilise ChatGPT for a similar thing"). - **Proactive alerting:** Wants the SC dashboard to highlight urgent items -- plans expiring, hours running low, funding period transitions. "Lots of prompting would be good. Prompting and highlighting."
- **Workflow integration concerns:** Notes friction of downloading from CRM to upload to BellaAssist ("I was just going to drag it across from my CRM"). Wants auto-population of outreach emails rather than manual copy-paste between screens.
- **Practical critique:** Identified specific issues in MVP -- evidence map missing diagnoses (ASD Level 3, cerebral palsy), outreach emails duplicating the same FCA request across multiple providers, generic compliance language unsuitable for GPs, need for in-line editing of generated reports.
